1. Overview

This dataset contains transactional and performance information for a network of retail stores.
It is designed for business analysis, performance benchmarking, and deep-dive investigations into what drives top-performing and under-performing stores.
The dataset enables users to evaluate profitability, sales volume, and product-level contributions across different stores.

3. Purpose of the Dataset
- The dataset is intended for:
Top/Bottom store analysis
Identify the best-performing and weakest stores using KPIs such as Profit, Revenue, Units, and Margin%.
Business diagnostics
Understand why certain stores outperform or underperform (pricing, margin, demand, product mix, etc.).

- Product-level contribution analysis
Evaluate which products drive profit in top stores vs bottom stores.

- Visualization dashboards
Build charts such as:
Dumbbell chart (Profit vs Margin%)
Top 5 / Bottom 5 comparison
KPI scorecards
Store ranking charts
Machine-learning preparation
Can be used for segmentation, forecasting, and anomaly detection.
